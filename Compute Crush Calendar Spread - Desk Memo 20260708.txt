**Strategy:** GPU Rental Term Structure – Calendar Spread (Focus: H200)

## 1. Trade Thesis
Sell rich front-month GPU rental futures and buy cheaper deferred-month contracts on the same chip (e.g., H200).  
Profit when the steep backwardation flattens as new GPU supply comes online while near-term demand (especially inference) keeps the front end supported.

This is a **relative value / statistical spread trade**, not purely market neutral. We are structurally short backwardation.

## 2. Current Market Conditions (Architect – July 8, 2026)
- **July (Front / Spot Month):** $3.20 per GPU-hour  
- **October (3-month):** $2.80 per GPU-hour  
- **Spread (July – October):** **40 cents**  
- **12-month:** $2.30

Front end remains rich due to strong on-demand / short-term needs from hyperscalers and AI labs.

## 3. Entry Rules (Both conditions required)
1. Long leg (deferred contract) is statistically **cheap** on historical percentile basis (high percentile = inexpensive).
2. Calendar spread is **wide** relative to its own historical distribution.

## 4. Corporate Litmus Test (CoreWeave + Crusoe)
Use this as the "pH balance" check against capital markets pricing.

**5-Factor Checklist** (Score 0–10 each, Total /50. Lower score = better for trade)

1. **Backlog Growth** (QoQ) – Rapid growth is negative for the trade.
2. **Near-term Utilization / Sold-Out Status** – 90%+ sold out for next 12–18 months is negative.
3. **New Contract Pricing Trend** – Lower $/GPU-hour on new deals is positive.
4. **Gross Margin on New Business** – Margin compression on fresh contracts is positive.
5. **Cluster Delivery / Supply Ramp** – Strong guidance on new GPUs coming online is positive.

**Scoring Interpretation:**
- < 25 → Strongly supportive of trade
- 25–35 → Neutral / mixed
- > 35 → Strongly against entering

## 5. Risk & Position Management ($10,000 Risk Allocation)
- **Profit Target:** Spread narrows to 15–20 cents → exit
- **Stop Loss:** Spread widens to 60–65 cents → exit
- **Time Stop:** Exit after 6–8 weeks if neither target/stop hit
- **Typical Holding Period:** 3–7 weeks
- Treat like Bitcoin basis: strict quant rules, no "it has to come back" mindset
